
---------------------------
Portofoliobro Documentation
---------------------------

Portfoliobro helps you visualise, simulate, and get purchase suggestions based on your portfolio.

Create your account, login, and add in your portfolio.

You can now do the following:
    - Visualise your existing portfolio
    - Add or remove stocks to your portfolio and see how it affects your risk profile
    - Get recommendations to align your portfolio with your risk appetite

How it works?
Beta is used as the metric to evaluate stock and portfolio volatility, which indicates risk with respect to the market
It is calculated using weekly close values of each stock over a 4 year period
The market index being tracked is the Nifty 50

We defined risk classes in the following ranges, by taking the possible range of beta values and classifying them:
    Very low - 0.0 to 0.5
    Low - 0.5 to 1.0
    Medium - 1.0 to 1.5
    High - 1.5 to 2.0
    Very High - Greater than 2.0
Negative beta values have the same ranges as positive ones, but they move opposite to the market.
